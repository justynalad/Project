Body Scan

Breathe in through your nose and out through the mouth. Breathe in deeply, feeling the air fill in your lungs, and breathe out. Focus on the air leaving your body. Do this five times. With the next breath focus your attention on the top of your head. Take a deep breath noticing any sensations. Move on to your face. Breathe in and out focusing on the sensations on your face. Repeat the same technique for the rest of your body. You can choose to focus on the smallest part of your body with each breath (nose, finger, belly button), or do a quicker scan of the whole (arm, leg, torso). With each body part focus on the sensation you are feeling. This could be slight pain, tingling, itching, or no sensation at all. When your mind wanders, do not beat yourself up.
